Under the auspices of the [North Bennington Outdoor Sculpture Show](https://www.nbossvt.com/), _Earthy_, a public art installation, has been selected for exhibition at the [Bennington Museum](https://benningtonmuseum.org/), June through September 2025. The show opens on June 28, 2025, from 4:00 to 8:00 pm, at 75 Main Street, Bennington, VT.

2026 will be busy as Elizabeth has a number of shows in the works. She is in a group show at [Jane Street Art Center](https://www.janestreetartcenter.com/) in Saugerties, NY, April 4 - May 10; is in a two-person show with artist [Chong Kang](https://chongkangartist.com/) at [Studio Gallery](https://www.studiogallerydc.com/) in Washington, DC in April; and has a solo show at [Convey/ER/OR](https://www.chronogram.com/hudsonvalley/convey-er-or-gallery/Location?oid=18954176), in October in Poughkeepsie, NY. There are always things brewing, so please stay tuned for more updates.
