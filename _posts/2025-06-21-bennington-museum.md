Under the auspices of the [North Bennington Outdoor Sculpture Show](https://www.nbossvt.com/), _Earthy_, a public art installation, has been selected for exhibition at the [Bennington Museum](https://benningtonmuseum.org/), June through November 2025. The show opens on June 28, 2025, from 4:00 to 8:00 pm, at 75 Main Street, Bennington, VT.

2026 will be busy as Elizabeth has a number of shows in the works. She is in a group show titled **TrashChic** at [Jane Street Art Center](https://www.janestreetartcenter.com/) in Saugerties, NY, April 4 - May 10 and a two-person show with artist [Chong Kang](https://chongkangartist.com/) at [Washington Center of ADA Gallery](https://adawdc.org/) in Washington, DC, also in April. She has 2 solo shows in October, one at [Convey/ER/OR](https://www.chronogram.com/hudsonvalley/convey-er-or-gallery/Location?oid=18954176) in Poughkeepsie, NY and the other at [Wallkill River Center For The Arts](https://wallkill.art/) in Montgomery, NY. There are always things brewing, so please stay tuned for more updates.
