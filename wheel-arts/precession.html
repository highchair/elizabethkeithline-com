Precession

Elizabeth’s curatorial proposal for Precession, an installation by artist Anne Morgan Spalter, was exhibited at the 2016 SPRING/BREAK Art Show, New York City’s curator-driven art fair during Armory Arts Week. The exhibit received lots of good press including:

Press

  • “Today is the Last Day to View Digital Artist Anne Spalter’s SPRING/BREAK Art Show Lobby Installation,” Adam Lehrer, Forbes. March 7, 2016. (Available in archive form from Archive.org)
  • “Spellbinding Pieces At The 2016 SPRING/BREAK Art Show,” Laura June Kirsch, The Village Voice. March 4, 2016. No longer available online.
  • “SPRING/BREAK’s Fifth Installment: The Coolest Show This Week,” Eliza Jordan, Whitewall Magazine. March 3, 2016. Not available online.
  • “SPRING/BREAK: A Sprawling Alternative Art Fair That Delivers,” Jillian Steinhauer, Hyperallergic. March 3, 2016. Not available online.
  • “Anne Spalter ‘Precession’ At SPRING/BREAK Art Show (pdf),” Kurt McVey, Whitehot Magazine. March 2016.
