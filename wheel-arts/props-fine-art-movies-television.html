Props & Fine Art From Movies, Television, & Theatre

At the Beard Gallery, Wheaton College, March 1 through April 13, 2018. Featuring artists Annie Atkins, Buist Bickley, Jay Duckworth, Eric B. Hart, Randy Lutz, and Carl Sprague.
