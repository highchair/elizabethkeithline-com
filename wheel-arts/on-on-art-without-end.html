On and On: Art Without End

On & On: Art Without End was originally shown at Mark Miller Gallery on Orchard Street in New York City, November 2015. It travelled to the Beard & Weil Galleries at Wheaton College and ran from January 24 through February 23, 2018.

Featuring the work of: Kim Bernard, Pati D’Amico, Patricia Fabricant, Ed Grant, Lynne Harlow, Tang Wei Hsu, Nicole Laemmle, Stephen Mallon, Dani & Kevin McCoy aka Work/Play, Anne Morgan Spalter, Carol Salmanson, Sean Smuda, Margot Spindelman, Max Van Pelt, William Warren, and Allan Wexler
