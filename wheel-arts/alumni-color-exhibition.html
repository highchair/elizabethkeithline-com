Alumni of Color Exhibition

The Alumni Of Color Exhibition was installed in the Madeleine Clark Wallace Library at Wheaton College in April 2018.

The exhibition presented a unique chance to work with students, administrators, and staff to research and further represent the activities of students of color at the college since its beginnings.
