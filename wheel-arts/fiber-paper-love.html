Fiber/Paper/Love

Fiber/Paper/Love was a group show installed in the Beard & Weil Gallery at Wheaton College from August 28 through November 3, 2018. The artists explored and interpreted fiber’s use and forms in innovative ways.

Participating artists: Catherine Evans, Aaron Pexa, Alyce Santoro, Cristin Searles, and Rebecca Siemering.
