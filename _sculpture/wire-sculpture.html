Wire Sculpture (various)

{% include picture.html filepath='assets/sculpture/wire-squiggly' alt="" %}

These sculptures were made by weaving wire around a form and burning it out. Keithline invented this technique in 1990 and called it “Lost Box”, a play on the term Lost Wax.

All photographs by Erik Gould.
