Lost House

{% include picture.html filepath='assets/sculpture/lost-house-01' alt="" %}

The Lost House Project was a full-scale lost dwelling exhibited at the Newport Art Museum in fall 2005. It was reviewed in the Providence Phoenix, the Nov/Dec 2005 Fiber Arts Magazine, and the Dec 2005 Sculpture Magazine.
